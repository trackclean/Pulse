Capabilities

Built for Producers

Ingest audio files, analyze them locally, and export curated sample packs — without ever modifying your originals.

Import Audio

Drag & drop files, use the file picker, or paste from your clipboard. Supports WAV, MP3, OGG, FLAC, M4A, AAC, and WMA.

Clean Your Samples

Scan for silent files and find exact duplicates. Remove them in one click. Your library stays lean and usable.

Detect Key & BPM

Analyze musical key with confidence scores and estimate BPM automatically. Edit both inline on every track.

Auto-Rename Files

Apply 10+ naming patterns using name, category, key, and BPM tokens. Preview every change before committing.

Tune & Analyze Pitch

Open the Chromatic Tuner on any sample. See dominant note, chroma spectrum, and top spectral peaks. Confirm or override the key.

Export Sample Packs

Export to folder or ZIP. Organize by category or keep flat. Only selected files or all non-silent files — your choice.

How It Works

Your Workflow, Streamlined

From import to export — everything runs on your machine.

01

Import & Analyze

Drag & drop files, use the file picker, or paste from clipboard. Pulse instantly decodes waveforms, detects duplicates via SHA-256, and estimates BPM. Each track shows its waveform, duration, key, BPM, and category — all editable inline.

Drag & Drop Waveform Decode SHA-256 Hashing BPM via Aubio
02

Categorize & Organize

Auto-categorization uses keyword matching against a fully customizable category system. Add, archive, or delete categories. Search, sort by name, import order, or group by category. Drag to manually reorder tracks.

Keyword Matching Custom Categories Search & Sort Manual Reorder
03

Clean & Rename

Analyze silence with RMS energy scanning. Remove silent files and duplicates in one click. Auto-rename with 10+ preset naming patterns or write custom ones using {name}, {category}, {key}, and {bpm} tokens. Full preview before applying.

Silence Analysis Duplicate Removal Naming Patterns Preview & Undo
04

Configure & Export

Fine-tune processing, display, playback, and export settings. Choose export as folder copy or ZIP archive with categorized or flat structure. Select naming presets or write custom patterns. Themes and waveform color overrides included.

Folder or ZIP Naming Presets Theme & Appearance Keyboard Shortcuts
05

Chromatic Tuner

Open the built-in tuner on any track for detailed pitch analysis. See the dominant note, frequency, and cents offset in real time. A chroma spectrum visualizes energy across all 12 notes, and the top 5 spectral peaks are listed by strength. Get a suggested key with confidence score, or manually select and confirm a key.

Pitch Analysis Chroma Spectrum Top 5 Peaks Manual Key Select
