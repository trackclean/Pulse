Clean Track Buddy

A local-first audio sample cleaner and organizer. Analyze, rename, categorize, and export your samples—all on your machine, all the time.

Download Now

Features

🔐

Local-First & Private

All analysis runs on your machine. No files are ever uploaded or shared.

🎯

Silence Detection

Automatically detect and remove silent or near-silent files from your packs.

🔄

Duplicate Detection

SHA-256 hashing identifies duplicates instantly. Clean up redundant samples.

🎵

Musical Key Detection

Optional analysis detects the musical key of each sample with confidence scores.

✏️

Smart Renaming

Auto-rename with custom patterns, keywords, and naming rules. Full preview before apply.

📦

Flexible Export

Export as folders or zip archives. Organize into categories or keep flat structure.
