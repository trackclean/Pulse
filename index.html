Built for Producers

Ingest audio files, analyze them locally, and export curated sample packs — without ever modifying your originals.

Import Audio

Drag & drop files, use the file picker, or paste from your clipboard. Supports WAV, MP3, OGG, FLAC, M4A, AAC, and WMA.

Clean Your Samples

Scan for silent files and find exact duplicates. Remove them in one click. Your library stays lean and usable.

Detect Key & BPM

Analyze musical key with confidence scores and estimate BPM automatically. Edit both inline on every track.

Auto-Rename Files

Apply 10+ naming patterns using name, category, key, and BPM tokens. Preview every change before committing.

Tune & Analyze Pitch

Open the Chromatic Tuner on any sample. See dominant note, chroma spectrum, and top spectral peaks. Confirm or override the key.

Export Sample Packs

Export to folder or ZIP. Organize by category or keep flat. Only selected files or all non-silent files — your choice.

Your Workflow, Streamlined

From import to export — everything runs on your machine.

01

Drop & Import

Drag & drop audio files onto the drop zone, use the file picker, or paste from your clipboard. Pulse supports WAV, MP3, OGG, FLAC, M4A, AAC, and WMA — all decoded instantly in-browser.

02

Analyze Tracks

Pulse instantly decodes waveforms, detects duplicates via SHA-256, and estimates BPM. Each track shows its waveform, duration, key, BPM, and category — all editable inline.

03

Clean & Rename

Analyze silence with RMS energy scanning. Remove silent files and duplicates in one click. Auto-rename with 10+ preset naming patterns or write custom ones using {name}, {category}, {key}, and {bpm} tokens. Full preview before applying.

04

Categorize & Organize

Auto-categorization uses keyword matching against a fully customizable category system. Add, archive, or delete categories. Search, sort by name, import order, or group by category. Drag to manually reorder tracks.

05

Configure Settings

Fine-tune processing, display, playback, and export behaviour. Toggle auto-rename on import, key and BPM detection, auto-categorization, notifications, and track display options — all from a single settings dialog.

06

Personalize

Choose from four built-in themes — Cyberpunk Neon, Midnight Blue, Deep Forest, and Warm Sunset. Each theme recolours the entire interface including waveforms. Override waveform colour independently with the custom colour picker.

07

Export

Export your curated sample pack as a folder copy or ZIP archive. Organize files into category subfolders or keep them flat. Export all non-silent files or only your selection — originals are never modified.
